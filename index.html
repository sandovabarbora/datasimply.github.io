Data, simply

Your business generates data every day. Sales, costs, customers, inventory.

It sits in spreadsheets, booking systems, payment platforms. Scattered. Hard to read. Impossible to act on.

It doesn't have to be this way.

Yes, this work often helps you make more money. I'm not pretending revenue doesn't matter — we all have bills to pay. But the real goal is giving you clarity so you can do what you actually want with your business, not what you feel forced to do because you're guessing. Whether that's growing, staying sustainable, or just having more time for the work that matters to you.

Common problems

You don't know what's working
Which product sells best? Which campaign brought customers? Is this month better than last month? The data exists, but you can't see it clearly.
Reports take hours every week
Copying numbers from five different systems. Making the same Excel sheet again and again. There's a better way — reports that update themselves.
Nothing talks to each other
Your sales system is separate from accounting. Your booking platform doesn't connect to inventory. Every number needs manual transfer.
Money disappears somewhere
Revenue looks good but profit doesn't. Costs keep growing but you're not sure where. You need to see exactly where money goes.

Common questions

Do I need to change my current systems?
Usually no. Most solutions work with what you already use — your existing spreadsheets, booking system, payment platform. If something needs changing, you'll know why and what it costs.
I'm not technical. Will I understand it?
If you can use email and open a spreadsheet, you're fine. Everything gets explained in plain language. No tech jargon, no assumptions.
How fast will you respond?
Usually within 24 hours on weekdays. First conversation is always free — just explain your problem and get a realistic answer about whether this can help.
What if it stops working?
Small fixes are free for the first month. After that, you can either fix simple things yourself (with the documentation) or ask for help at an hourly rate.
Can I change it later myself?
Depends on the solution. Simple Excel automations? Probably yes. Complex integrations? Probably not without help. You'll know upfront what you can and can't modify.
Do I need an IT person?
No. That's the point. Solutions are built to work without technical support. If your nephew who "knows computers" leaves, things keep working.
